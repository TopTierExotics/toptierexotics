FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

This section closes trust gaps, reduces repetitive DMs, and gives the brand a more serious front-facing presentation.

Why is Telegram the main access point? +

Telegram is used for early release notices, quantity updates, and direct support. It keeps the highest-intent buyers closest to the drop schedule.

Can I order from outside the UK? +

Yes. Worldwide shipping support is available depending on destination and carrier coverage. Confirm location details before payment.

How do I know sizing before I buy? +

Message before checkout for size guidance, fit notes, and availability. That conversation should happen before payment so the order stays clean.

Are all products permanently available? +

No. The brand position is built around controlled quantity, selected releases, and lower-volume availability rather than permanent open inventory.
